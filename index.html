About Me

I was born in Sydney, Australia and moved to the States when I turned 8. I don't remember much of my time in Australia and I don't have any accent at all. Afterwards, I was raised in Bergen County, New Jersey and attended Bergen County Academies as a student in their CS program. Back then, I did a bunch of hackathons and cs olympiad competitions which helped get me interested in the field.

In my free time, I like to play video games like Dota 2, Path of Exile, League of Legends, and osu! to name a few. I used to be ranked top 100 in US on the osu! ladder. I also follow the smash (melee) scene and I'm trying to get better at my Capt. Falcon. Outside of video games, I am also a Lion Dancer and Chinese diaboloist. I'm also an active brother of Lambda Chi Alpha (LXA).

Now I'm currently in my third year of college studying computer science at University of Pennsylvania. My interests are in performance based computing, graphics, virtual/augmented reality, and a bit of ML. I also have an interest in 3D Modeling in Maya and usually stream myself doing work on Twitch.

Personal Projects

I spend a lot of time outside of school and work building fun apps. Here's a couple that I want to highlight.

Cuda Path Tracer

A realtime Path-Tracer written in C++/Cuda. Built for performance.

Obstruction Free Photography

Computer vision research paper implemented in C++/CUDA from scratch. Built for performance.

Egyptian Rat Screw

Low-latency reaction based card game with game networking implemented over WebSockets. Played on mobile and uses desktop as viewport.

Butler

A RESTful API microservice that scrapes trivia off the Dota 2 wiki site and serves them. Aggregation uses ML and NLP techniques to identify and clean trivia questions.

Slack bot packed with game logic to play a game of Dota 2 Trivia. It is inspired by the bot living in #trivia in Dota 2.

Dominion

A command line simulation of the popular card game Dominion written in pure Haskell.

Mind Palace

Top 30 PennApps hack on the VR platform that transports you to a meditation room and surrounds you with your fondest memories.

Procedural Park

Maya plugin to help initialize a procedurally generated park based on a series of convex optimizations.
